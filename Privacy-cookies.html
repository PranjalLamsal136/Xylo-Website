Privacy Policy & Cookies – XYLO

At XYLO, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

You are responsible for keeping your account password secure. XYLO is not responsible for unauthorized access caused by weak passwords or sharing login details.

Online payments are handled securely through trusted services like eSewa, Khalti, and Bank Transfer. XYLO does not store your payment passwords or sensitive financial data.

XYLO uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies help us to:

  • Understand how users use our website

You can disable cookies from your browser settings, but some features of the site may not work properly.

We may use third-party services for payment processing and delivery. These services have their own privacy policies, and XYLO is not responsible for their practices.

7. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal data from misuse, loss, or unauthorized access.

8. Changes to This Policy

XYLO may update this Privacy & Cookies Policy at any time. Continued use of the website means you accept the updated policy.
