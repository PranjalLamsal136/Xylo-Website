Accessibility – XYLO

XYLO is committed to making our website accessible and easy to use for everyone, including people with disabilities.

1. Our Commitment

We aim to provide a website that can be accessed by all users, regardless of device, ability, or technology.

2. Accessibility Features

  • Clear and readable fonts
  • Proper color contrast for text and background
  • Simple navigation and layout
  • Images with descriptive alt text
  • Keyboard-friendly navigation
  • Mobile and desktop responsiveness

3. Ongoing Improvements

We continuously work to improve the accessibility of our website and fix any issues that may affect user experience.

4. Third-Party Content

Some features like payment gateways (eSewa, Khalti, Bank services) are managed by third parties. We cannot fully control their accessibility, but we choose widely trusted services.

5. Need Help?

If you face any difficulty while using our website or have suggestions to improve accessibility, please contact us at: xylo@gmail.com
